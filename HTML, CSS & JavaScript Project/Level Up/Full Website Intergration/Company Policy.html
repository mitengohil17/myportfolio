    What are Company Policies?

    Company policies are guidelines that help employers deal with the health, safety and accountability of employees.

    as well as their interactions with customers or clients.

    A workplace safety policy is now essential within all companies large and small. If the workplace is not safe, in this day and age you will most likely taken to court for it.Therefore,it is intrinsic that there is a policy firm inplace to protect not just employee but the company.

    There may be all sorts of reasons an employee may need to be absent from work such as annual leave or longstanding leave. However for each reason there is different treament and there will be written in place rules to prevent ambiguity for each employee.
